Scrolling Issues?

This game requires scrolling to use the loadout. Click onto the game to lock in, and click out to unlock!

Controls

WASD: Move

1-5 or scroll wheel: Loadout slots

F: Interact

X: Activate ultimate (once heat bar is ready)

Space (while moving): Roll

E: Parry

R: Reload

Left click: Fire

Right click: Alt Fire (only some weapons)
